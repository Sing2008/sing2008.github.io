Commentary Speaking

The Rules

Competitors have 30 minutes to prepare a 5-minute speech, with a 30 second grace period, on a current events or social issue topic. Unlike Extemp and Impromptu, the speech is delivered while being seated, simulating a news or talk show commentary. Competitors are allowed to use a notecard during the speech.
