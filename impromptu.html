Impromptu Speaking

The Rules

Competitors are given a random topic, such as a quote, word, or abstract idea. They have 7 minutes in total to prepare and deliver a speech. The event consists of a 30 second grace period. The skill lies in time managment and quick thinking. The speech is typically structured into an introduction, two or three main points, and a conclusion.
